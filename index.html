How to use Dolphin Clicker

  1. Add a Clicker Profile: Click on the "New Clicker" button. Select whether you want to automate mouse clicks or keyboard key strokes.
  2. Configure Settings:
    • Choose your trigger action (Left Click, Right Click, or a custom keyboard key).
    • Specify the spamming speed. You can input the speed either in Interval (ms) or Speed (Clicks Per Second). The program will auto-calculate the other.
    • Set a custom toggle hotkey (e.g. F6 or F9).
  3. Toggle Clicking: Press the configured hotkey while in-game or in another window. The clicker will start/stop immediately in the background. You can also click the play/stop icon on the dashboard card.
  4. Stop All Instantly: Press F8 (default master hotkey) or click the **"Stop All"** button at the top header to immediately stop all running clicking scripts.
